Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers to common questions about using the FindIt platform.

How do I report a found item?

To report a found item, click on the "Report Found Item" button on the homepage or navigate to the Report section. Fill out the form with details about the item, upload clear photos, and specify where and when you found it. Once submitted, the item will be added to our database and visible to users searching for lost items.

How long are items kept before being donated?

All found items are kept for 60 days from the date they're reported. If unclaimed after this period, items are donated to local charities or handled according to campus policy. Valuable items like electronics, jewelry, and official documents are kept for up to 90 days.

What proof do I need to claim my item?

To claim an item, you'll need to provide specific details that only the owner would know (such as distinctive marks, scratches, content details, or passwords for electronics). You'll also need to show your campus ID. For high-value items, additional verification may be required.

Are there rewards for returning found items?

Our platform operates on goodwill rather than rewards, but item owners may choose to offer rewards voluntarily. We track and acknowledge our most active contributors through our community recognition program, which highlights those who consistently help return lost items.

How do I get notified if my item is found?

When you report a lost item, you can set up alerts for items matching your description. Our system will automatically notify you via email or push notification when a similar item is reported found. You can also check your dashboard regularly for potential matches.
